# Philadelphia Flood Risk Map

**Subtitle:** Overlay of critical infrastructure and flood zones

**Tools:** QGIS

**Data Sources:**
- FEMA Flood Map Service Center
- OpenDataPhilly

**Description:** 
This project visualizes **flood risk zones in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania**, based on FEMAâ€™s National Flood Hazard Layer. The data was clipped to the Philadelphia city boundary and styled in **QGIS** to highlight areas with varying flood risk levels. Additional layers include **bus transit shelters, hospitals, schools, and PA rail lines** for context.

**Purpose:**
- Practice GIS mapping in QGIS
- Integrating multiple spatial datasets
- Styling and symbolizing flood risk data
- Designing a clear, readable thematic map
